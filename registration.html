Online Registration Steps

Google Chrome is most preferred Brower for the registration.

  1. Login into the system with your login ID sand Password

  2. Please Review and edit your personal information and make sure it is up to date.You are to verify ,fill,
    and compare all required feilds as shown on this page.Click on update profile & contiune once you have
    completed this stage

  3. Please Reviewand edit detailsfor the programme you are currently pursing and make sure it is up to
    date . you are to verify, fill and complete all required feilds as shown on this page. click on update profile
    & contiune once you have completed this stage

  4. At the Options page , please select Online registration for semester registration. Please ensure to read
    the instructions at the next level . Proceed to course Registration.

  5. At the top left, you may click the buttom to register your trailed course(if any) and compulsory
    course for the semester will be automatically selected for you by the system. Select your department
    electives if any. You may select any oprn elective(VC's Course Regidtration.

  6. Click "save" and print registration slip to complete the registrationbuttom.On clicking the buttom, you
    are prompted for the pop-up settings for the browers, please allow pop-upto enable you to print your slip.
